Home page

1. Open the index.html file in FireFox

2. At the top of the main page you will see our logo and project name. There are also a few links, one will redirect you to your map where you can shop for things and view your inventory.

3. Below the logo and title you will see a dawg environment which is where you can see your dawg in its environment!

4. Below the dawg environment you will see a partially functional calendar table area where you can create tasks and assign start and due dates for each one. Upon entering the task name, start date, and due date, you can click on the green add task button to add your task to the calendar table

5. You can change weeks and view different weeks by clicking on the green buttons above the calendar table

Map

1. Open the map by clicking on the "Map" link in the page header!

2. Upon entering the map, you will see multiple building images which you can click on to navigate the various map locations!

3. In each of these locations you can purchase items with your dawg points!

4. If you enter your inventory, you can swap currently equipped items or equip your new purchases!

5. Click on the dawg logo at any time to navigate back to your home page!
